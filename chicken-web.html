Introduction

Poultry farming is the process of raising domesticated birds such as chickens for the purpose of farming meat or eggs for food.
Chicken farming is one of the most common types of poultry farming and can be highly rewarding.

Benefits of Chicken Farming

  • Source of fresh eggs and meat
  • Low startup costs
  • Good for small spaces or backyards
  • Helps in pest control and fertilizing the soil

Types of Chickens

There are many chicken breeds, each suited for different purposes:

  • Layers: Raised mainly for egg production
  • Broilers: Raised for meat
  • Dual-purpose: Good for both eggs and meat (improved kienyeji)
